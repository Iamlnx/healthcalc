What BMI stands for?

BMI means Body Mass Index, it is a calculated measurement of body weight relative to height.

How it is calculated?

BMI = Weight(Kg) / Height(m)²

Does it count to anyone?

No, BMI is intended for adults only, as children and adolescents are constantly growing. For adults, BMI is categorized into underweight, healthy weight, overweight, and obesity.

It tell if i’m healthy or not?

BMI doesn't directly measure body fatness, but it is correlated with direct measures of adiposity. BMI also doesn't differentiate between lean body mass and fat mass. For example, a person can have a high BMI due to being muscular but still have a low fat mass. Always contact a health professional to be sure!
